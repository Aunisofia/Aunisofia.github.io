Technology and Information System

Information systems refers to the use of technology to manage and analyze data, facilitate decision-making processes, and support organizational operations. Information systems technology is essentially a combination of both IT and IS into one diverse field. This unique branch of tech delves into the integral aspects of both IS and IT. More specifically, information systems technology is going to cover both the development of these systems as well as the technology that keeps them in place.
